There are thousands of chemicals in your products, many of which are being absorbed into your body. The effects of the chemicals can be very damaging in the long run. Many cosmetic products contain parabens, synthetic colors, fragrances, formaldehyde, phthalates, sodium lauryl sulfates, and many more. These dangerous chemicals not only damage your largest organ, your skin, they also affect your insides. Sodium lauryl sulfates can cause a host of issues like kidney and respiratory damage. Parabens and phthalates have connections to the increase risk of breast cancer. Fragrance mixes have been associated with allergies, dermatitis, respiratory distress and possible effects on the reproductive system.
